Demand Forecasting

Forecast Methods Utilised

Method Applicability
Simple Exponential Smoothing (SES) The SES forecasting method is utilised to forecast demand that has no outright trend or seasonality. Thus, it is approriate to use for demand that varies period to period with no noticeable trend or seasonality.
Holt's Model Holt's model forecasting method is utilised to forecast demand that has a visible trend but no seasonality. Thus, it is approriate to use for demand that has a visible upward or downward trajectory with no noticeable seasonality.
Winter's Model Winter's model forecasting method is utilised to forecast demand that has a visible trend and seasonality. Thus, it is appropriate to use for demand that has a visible upward or downward trajectory combined with a noticeable seasonality.
